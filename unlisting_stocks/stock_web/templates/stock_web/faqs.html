What are Pre IPO Stocks?
Pre IPO - An offering of shares of a company before its Initial Public Offer. i.e. before they get listed on the Stock Exchanges.
What are Unlisted Stocks?
Unlisted Stocks are share that are currently not listed on any Stock Exchange and are available to trade in the secondary market.
How can I invest in Pre PIO & Unlisted Stocks?
You can invest in the stocks that fit your portfolio & match your risk appetite by directly purchasing them from Trade Unlisted. Our team will be happy to help you the right stocks.

What is time horizon for investing in Pre IPO & Unlisting Stocks?
Most Pre IPO & Unlisting Stocks take 2 to 4 years to get listed as it involves processes like - Shareholders Approval, Getting internal processes in order, Hiring Merchant Bankers, Filing for DRHP, Gettig approval SEBI etc, We Don't guarantee the listing of stocks..
What is the minimum amountone can invest in Pre IPO & Unlisting Stocks?
The minimum investment starts from INR 555. However, it depends on the current stocks price.
Is there any lock-in period for Pre IPO & Unlisted Stocks?
There is no lock-in period to listing, You can buy or sell your shares in the secondary market. There is a lock-in for 1 year from the date of listing.
Where are my Per IPO & Unlisted Stocks Held?
Your stocks are held in your CDSL or NSDL Demat Accounts. If you don't have one, we will help you open it.
What are the Tax Implications of investing in Pre IPO & Unlisting Stocks?
Pre IPO & Unlisted Stocks are taxed as short or long term capital gain and enjoy indexation benefit. LTCG on sale of Unlisted Stocks is taxable at 10% without indexation and 20% with idexation. STCG on sale of Unlisted Stocks is application marginal tax rate. We still advise you to consult your taxation expert.
